curriculum vitae

Postdoc, Dartmouth College

I am a postdoc in the Department of Mathematics at Dartmouth College, working with Profs. Anne Gelb and Yoonsang Lee. I received my Ph.D. from Southern Methodist University working with Prof. Wei Cai. I obtained my B.S. in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics at Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

My research interests lie in applied and computational mathematics for artificial intelligence (AI), scientific machine learning, scientific computing, mathematics of machine learning and inverse problems. I develop advanced fast machine learning methods to understand complex physical phenomena and solve engineering problems (e.g. waves, fluid dynamics, combustion).

Check out my Research Page and Google Scholar for more details. I will be on academic job market in Fall 2025. Feel free to reach out by email if you share similar interests!
